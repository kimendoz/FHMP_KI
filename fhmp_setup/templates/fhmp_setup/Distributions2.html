The specific likelihoods in our “distribution of outcomes around #” mean the following:

  • There is a 30% chance the asset will be worth #
  • There is a 18% chance the asset will be worth # + 1
  • There is a 18% chance the asset will be worth # - 1
  • There is a 2% chance the asset will be worth any other number from 1 to 20*

*Note: Possible values are 1-20, so if # is either 1 or 20, then the last line is "There is a 3% chance the asset will be worth any other number from 1 to 20".
